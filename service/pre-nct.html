Pre-NCT

At Cork Motorz Centre, we provide thorough pre-NCT checks in Cork to help ensure your vehicle is fully prepared for its National Car Test. Our goal is to identify and fix any potential issues before your NCT appointment, reducing the risk of failure and saving you time and money.

Complete NCT Preparation Service

Our experienced technicians carry out a detailed inspection of your vehicle, checking all key safety and emissions components to ensure they meet NCT standards. We highlight any issues that may cause a failure and offer clear, cost-effective solutions to get your car ready for retest.


What We Check During a Pre-NCT Inspection

Our comprehensive pre-NCT inspection in Cork includes checks on:

  • Brakes and braking efficiency
  • Tyres and tread depth
  • Suspension and steering components
  • Lights and indicators
  • Exhaust and emissions system
  • Windscreen, wipers, and washers
  • Engine warning lights and diagnostics
