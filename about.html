About Us

At Cork Motorz Centre, we pride ourselves on delivering reliable, high-quality automotive services to drivers throughout Cork. Conveniently located on Watercourse Road, just minutes from Cork City Centre, we provide a complete range of vehicle care solutions under one roof.

Our experienced technicians specialise in vehicle servicing, mechanical repairs, advanced auto diagnostics, DPF cleaning, catalytic converter and intercooler cleaning, crash repairs, and general maintenance for all makes and models. Whether your vehicle needs routine servicing, fault diagnosis, engine repairs, or specialist cleaning to restore performance, we're committed to delivering professional workmanship using quality equipment and proven techniques.

In addition to repairs and servicing, we also offer vehicle recovery services with our dedicated recovery truck, ensuring help is available when you need it most. To keep your vehicle looking as good as it performs, we provide professional car washing and valeting services, leaving every vehicle clean, refreshed, and road-ready.

At Cork Motorz Centre, we understand how important your vehicle is to your everyday life. That's why we focus on honest advice, transparent pricing, and efficient service, helping you get back on the road safely and with confidence. Our commitment to quality workmanship and excellent customer care has made us a trusted choice for motorists across Cork seeking dependable, affordable automotive services.
