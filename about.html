About Us

At Cork Motorz Centre, we pride ourselves on delivering reliable, high-quality automotive services to drivers across Cork. Conveniently located on Watercourse Road, we offer a wide range of services including vehicle repairs, diagnostics, car washing and detailing, and breakdown recovery assistance.

Our team is dedicated to keeping your vehicle running smoothly and safely, whether you need routine maintenance, urgent repairs, or fast roadside recovery. We understand how important your car is to your daily life, which is why we focus on providing efficient service, honest advice, and dependable workmanship.

With a strong reputation for friendly service and customer satisfaction, Cork Motorz Centre continues to be a trusted choice for motorists looking for professional and affordable automotive care.
